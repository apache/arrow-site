## Changelog

### What's Changed
* fix(internal): fix assertion on undefined behavior by @amoeba in https://github.com/apache/arrow-go/pull/602
* ci(benchmark): switch to new conbench instance by @rok in https://github.com/apache/arrow-go/pull/593
* fix(flight): make StreamChunksFromReader ctx aware and cancellation-safe by @arnoldwakim in https://github.com/apache/arrow-go/pull/615
* fix(parquet/variant): fix basic stringify by @zeroshade in https://github.com/apache/arrow-go/pull/624
* fix(parquet/pqarrow): fix partial struct panic by @zeroshade in https://github.com/apache/arrow-go/pull/630
* Flaky test fixes by @zeroshade in https://github.com/apache/arrow-go/pull/629
* ipc: clear variadicCounts in recordEncoder.reset() by @asubiotto in https://github.com/apache/arrow-go/pull/631
* fix(arrow/cdata): Handle errors to prevent panic by @xiaocai2333 in https://github.com/apache/arrow-go/pull/614
* chore: bump version to 18.5.1 by @zeroshade in https://github.com/apache/arrow-go/pull/632
